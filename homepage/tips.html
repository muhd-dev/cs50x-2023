Tips & Resources!

Here are some tips to help you succeed in the course:

  1. Start with Scratch to grasp fundamental concepts.
  2. Don't be afraid to ask questions in the forums or seek help from teaching staff.
  3. Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.
  4. Practice coding daily to reinforce your skills.
  5. Join study groups or online communities for support.
  6. Use the CS50 IDE for coding; it's set up for the course.
  7. Review lecture notes and problem set instructions thoroughly.
  8. Strive to solve problem sets on your own before seeking online resources.
  9. Respect the course's ethics; avoid copying code from the internet.
