Who am i?

Hi, I'm Muhammad Idoniwako, a Full-Stack Developer, Designer, and Advisor passionate about people, innovation, creative problem-solving, and lifelong learning, hailing from Nigeria, who embarked on my coding journey in June 2023 after a career as an Ace Photographer for almost a decade transitioning into the realm of technology.

What is CS50x?

CS50x is a free online version of Harvard University's introductory computer science course, known for its comprehensive curriculum covering various aspects of computer science and programming taught by David J. Malan.

Languages you will learn??

You'll commence your journey with Scratch, which isn't a programming language but serves as an excellent initial step to acquaint you with fundamental programming concepts. Following that, you'll dive into the study of languages such as C, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as topics like security and more.

What's interesting about this Course?

What drew me the most to enroll in CS50 was Professor David J. Millan, whose teaching style is simply outstanding, and the infectious energy he brings to each lecture keeps you engaged throughout; in addition, the course preceptors ike Brain, Doug, and others are exceptionally talented.

What i think about the Problem Sets?

The problem sets are of very challenging; but true learning occurs when you tackle them independently. Although they can be quite challenging, but the sense of accomplishment and a rush of dopamine you experience upon solving them is immensely rewarding. While it's acceptable to seek online resources when you're stuck, it's vital to uphold the course's academy honesty by refraining from directly obtaining the answers online.

My Advice?

Don't allow yourself to become overwhelmed; if you encounter a challenging problem, consider leaving it for the next day and continue making progress. Additionally, you can find plenty of helpful resources on my Tips page or GitHub, where I'll be sharing valuable insights and guidance.
